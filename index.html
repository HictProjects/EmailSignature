How to create/change your signature in Outlook 2010?

  1. Fill out your data and click the "Generate" button
  2. Select the full or short signature and copy to the clipboard
  3. Open Outlook
  4. Click File > Options > Mail > Signatures ...
  5. Create a new signature
  6. Paste the data
  7. Modify if needed
  8. Click the save button
  9. Modify your default signature setting if needed
  10. Click OK to close the signature window
